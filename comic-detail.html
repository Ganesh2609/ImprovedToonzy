When the elements of the game start manifesting in reality, humans better be ready to face the challenges. And they have to keep getting stronger. As the only Necromancer in the world, Lin Meyu will shine as long as the summoned creatures don't die out.

In a world where game mechanics suddenly become real, players must adapt or perish. Lin Meyu, once an ordinary gamer, now wields the rare and feared power of Necromancy. As he navigates this new reality, he'll discover that raising the dead comes with consequences he never imagined.

About the Author

Raven Studios is a collaborative team of artists and writers dedicated to creating immersive fantasy and action comics. With a focus on detailed worldbuilding and character development, they've become known for their unique blend of Eastern and Western comic styles.

Founded in 2021, Raven Studios has published 12 different series, with "The Necromancer" being their most popular work to date. The team consists of lead writer Alex Chen, illustrator Mei Zhang, and colorist Jordan West.
